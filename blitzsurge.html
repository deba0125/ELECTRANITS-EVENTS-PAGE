BLITZ-SURGE
“Life is a long journey so enjoy the journey and make some beautiful memories along the way”
Feeling stressed about the big mess that online classes have created? Sitting in front of computers , confined within the four walls , looming deadlines ,never ending assignments and what not. This journey is never too easy but still we can have a break and enjoy it and quit worrying about the consequences. That is why , to add more fun and excitement to this draggy vacation , Electra Society brings to you BLITZ-SURGE, a profusion of electrifying events, where you can relish yourselves and bring out your creative power and inquisitive mind. So come to light, showcase your talent and rejoice together on this amazing and indelible journey from 9th to 15th August, 2021.
